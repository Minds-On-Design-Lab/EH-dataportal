Emergency department visits for asthma
About the data

Asthma syndrome is identified in emergency department visit data by looking for chief complaints (descriptions of why patients are at the ED) that mention mention of asthma, wheezing, airway, or chronic obstructive pulmonary disorder (COPD).

A syndromes is a group of signs (something a health care provider can observe) and symptoms (something a patient feels) that are often associated with a particular disease or condition. It's not the same as a medical diagnosis.
